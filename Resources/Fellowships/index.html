Fellowships

Below is a list of undergraduate and graduate scholarships and fellowships I’ve collected over the years. I will add to and update this list continually, though some of the information may become outdated between updates.

Undergraduate Scholarships

  • Gates Millenium Scholarship
    Target group: underrepresented minorities in all disciplines
  • United Negro College Fund
    Target group: African Americans (all disciplines)
  • Great Minds in STEM
    Target group: Hispanics in STEM fields
  • American Indian Science and Engineering Society
    Target group: Native Americans and other indiginous peoples in STEM fields
  • CODE2040
    Target group: black and latino/a students in tech (graduate fellowships also available)
Graduate Fellowships (all students)
  • NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (NSF GRFP)
  • Hertz Foundation Fellowship
  • National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate Fellowship (NDSEG)
  • Computational Science Graduate Fellowship (CSGF)
  • National Physical Science Consortium (NPSC)
Graduate Fellowships (specific groups)
  • GEM Fellowship
    Target group: women and underrepresented minorities in STEM fields
  • Anita Borg scholarship
    Target group: women in tech
  • Society of Women Engineers (SWE)
    Target group: women in all engineering disciplines
  • Semiconductor Research Corporation
    Target group: women and underrepresented minorities in semiconductor-related fields
Graduate Fellowships (abroad)
  • Rhodes Scholarship
    Location: Oxford University
  • Marshall Scholarship
    Location: any university in the United Kingdom
  • Fulbright Fellowship
    Location: participating university
  • Schwarzman Scholars
    Location: Tsinghua University, Beijing, China
